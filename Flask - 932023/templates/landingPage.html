Document Summarization

Document summarization is the process of creating a shorter version of a longer text document while retaining its main ideas and important details. The purpose of summarization is to make the document more accessible and easier to understand, especially when dealing with large volumes of text.

This involves selecting the most important sentences or paragraphs from the original document and combining them to create a summary. Extractive summarization is often used in news articles or scientific papers.

Abstractive Summarization

This involves creating a summary by understanding the meaning of the text and then rephrasing it in a shorter form. Abstractive summarization requires natural language processing and machine learning techniques and is more challenging than extractive summarization. It is often used for summarizing longer articles or documents.
